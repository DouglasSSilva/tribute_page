Mr. Winston Churchill

"We are all worms, but I do believe that I am a glow-worm"

Here's a time line of Mr. Churchill's life:

1- 1874 born in Oxfordshire, England

2- 1895 joins Fourth Queen's Own Hussars serving in the Indian northwest frontier and the Sudan

3- 1898 "The Story of the Malakand Field Force" first book of his authorship describing his experiences in the army

4- 1899 "The River War" second book of his authorship describing his experiences in the army

5- 1900 Turns a member of the British Parliament in the Conservative Party for Oldham

6- 1904 switches to the liberal party

7- 1911 Named first lord of the Admiralty helps to modernize the british army

8- 1940-1945 Period Churchill worked as England Prime Minister
